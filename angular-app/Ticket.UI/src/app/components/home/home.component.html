Welcome to TicketApp

Your Destination for Concert Tickets

Experience the thrill of live music and entertainment with TicketApp. Discover, purchase, and enjoy tickets to your favorite concerts and events.

No account required. Easy ticket retrieval by name. Unforgettable experiences await you.

Key Features

  • Explore and buy tickets for top events.
  • No sign-up required for quick purchases.
  • Effortless ticket retrieval with your name.
  • Admin tools for event management.
  • Stay updated with the latest events.

Technologies Used

TicketApp is powered by modern technologies to provide with a seamless ticket buying experience:

  • Frontend: Angular
  • Frontend Framework: Bootstrap
  • Backend: ASP.NET
  • Database: PostgreSQL
